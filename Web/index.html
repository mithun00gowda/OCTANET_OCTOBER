"Cool the Globe, Save Our Home."

Global warming is an urgent global crisis driven by the excessive release of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere, primarily from human activities such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation. These gases trap heat, causing Earth's average temperature to rise steadily. The consequences are far-reaching, including more frequent and severe heatwaves, rising sea levels due to melting polar ice, disrupted weather patterns, and heightened risks to ecosystems and biodiversity. Vulnerable communities face food and water shortages, displacement, and increased climate-related disasters. To address this crisis, worldwide efforts are essential, emphasizing renewable energy adoption, sustainable practices, and carbon emissions reduction to secure a sustainable future for our planet.

About when Gobal warming happend
